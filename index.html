FAQ

💻 What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is an fun tech event where teams collaborate on a project, or "hack", within a weekend. There will be workshops and mentors to help you learn new things and get help on your projects.

👩‍💻 Who is eligible?

Hydra Hacks is for people of marginalized genders who have been historically underrepresented in technology. The minimum age to participate is 13 years by May 28, 2023.

📚 What if I don't have experience?

Our hackathon is beginner friendly! There will be workshops, mentors, and opportunities to learn new things and get help on your projects!

⚖️ Who will be judging?

Projects will be judged by industry experts from Bay Area tech companies. Judges will provide attendees with feedback, recognition, and prizes for their project.

🎒 What should I bring?

A computer, charger, and a positive mindset. If you don't have a computer, please email for a loaner laptop. Everything else will be provided, including meals (breakfast, lunch, dinner, snacks).

🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Do I need a team?

While you do have the option to hack alone, we highly encourage participants to form teams. At the beginning, we'll have team formation activities and make sure no hacker is left out.

⌚ Am I required to stay for the entire event?

No, you are welcome to leave whenever you want! If you are under the age of 18, your guardian has to sign a form (will be sent out closer to the hackathon's date.)

💸 Do I have to pay?

The hackathon is free, thanks to our generous sponsors!
